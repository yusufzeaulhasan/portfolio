Two-Stage CMOS Operational Amplifier

Cadence Virtuoso • 45 nm • stability + performance verification

← Back Contact

Overview

Designed and simulated a two-stage CMOS operational amplifier in a 45 nm process using Cadence Virtuoso. Implemented a fully differential first stage with common-mode feedback (CMFB) and a common-source second stage. Verified DC, AC, and transient performance including stability and slew behavior.

Target Specs

  • High unity-gain bandwidth (UGB)
  • Reasonable phase margin for stability
  • Low power under process constraints

Architecture

  • Stage 1: fully differential input stage
  • CMFB loop for stable output common-mode
  • Stage 2: common-source gain stage

Results

  • 47.5 dB DC gain
  • 616 MHz unity-gain frequency
  • 61\u00B0 phase margin

Verification (what to show)

  • DC: bias points, operating regions, output swing
  • AC: open-loop gain/phase, UGB, phase margin, poles/zeros
  • Transient: step response, settling time, overshoot
  • Slew: large-signal behavior under load
